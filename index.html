Learn About Triangles

How much do you know about triangles?

A triangle is a polygon with three edges and three vertices. It is one of the basic shapes in geometry.

There are 3 types of triangles

An Equilateral Triangle is a triangle whose
3 sides are equal.

An Isosceles Triangle is a triangle whose only
2 sides are equal.

A Scalene Triangle is a triangle in which none of the sides are equal.
